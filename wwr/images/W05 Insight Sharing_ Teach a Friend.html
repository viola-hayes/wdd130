W05 Insight Sharing: Teach a Friend

  • Due Saturday by 11:59pm
  • Points 10
  • Submitting a file upload
  • File Types doc and docx
  • Available Jan 27 at 12am - Feb 24 at 11:59pm

Overview

In this activity, you will teach a principle or skill you have learned in this unit and share insights that you gained through the experience. You will then write about your experiences and insights. The purpose of this activity is to deepen your understanding through teaching and sharing these insights with others. This also blesses the lives of those whom you teach and share with (3 Nephi 12:14–16 Links to an external site.). Your instructor will grade your submission and provide feedback.

Instructions

Note: Even though this assignment isn't due until the end of the week, it is important that you get started on this activity early in the week. If you are meeting face-to-face, you will need to set up a time to meet. If you are sharing via social media, email, etc., you will need to post early in the week to give time for conversation to happen.

  1. Prepare: Reflect on what you have learned during this unit. Identify meaningful insights from your study and research this week that inspired you the most. Ponder (1) why they are important to you or (2) how they apply in your life.
  2. Teach: Choose one or two principles or insights that you have learned this week and teach those things to another individual. This person may be a family member, coworker, friend, or anyone else that is not currently enrolled in this course. Here are some ideas for how to do this:
    • Teach in-person with a friend, neighbor, or family member—individually or in a group (family council, FHE, etc.).
    • Teach via a phone call, Skype call, or web meeting.
    • Teach in a chatroom, email conversation, social media post, or blog post—then interact in the conversation.
    • Teach in a video recording and share it with friends. Solicit comments and interact with the conversation.
    • Write a letter to someone who doesn't have access to technology.
    • Others you can think of.
  3. Report: Download the Teach a Friend Report template Download Teach a Friend Report template
    Minimize File Preview
    , and answer the provided questions. Please save your work and upload it into this assignment.

For example, you may want to teach about one of the following prompts:

  • Share one of the most interesting things you discovered this week in studying the Abrahamic covenant. You may want to consider sharing how the Abrahamic covenant helps you better understand one of the following:
    • The importance of temple marriage.
    • The declaration of lineage in a patriarchal blessing.
    • The significance of being born in the covenant.
    • The emphasis on every worthy young man in the Church serve an honorable full-time mission.
    • The Savior and His infinite Atonement.
  • Include a question or concern you might have about what you studied in the Abrahamic covenant.
